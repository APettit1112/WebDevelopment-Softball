Introduction to Softball

Softball is played all over the world, but it’s not as highly recognized as some of the other sports such as baseball or football. Softball has gained more popularity over the years as more women take part in softball.

Key Components of the Game

When playing the sport there are many key components to think about, such as what position someone plays, what's the batter’s count and what they can do, and how many can be on the field and the average size of a team.
